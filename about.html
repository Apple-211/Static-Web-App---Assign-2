About This Website

Welcome!

This website was created for people to learn about a few characters created by Disney. Disney characters range from villains and princesses to well-known superheroes.

Why Disney Characters?

I chose Disney characters because there are so many with unique abilities and different backstories that are loved by people of all ages.

The goal of this website is to provide a simple gallery where users can learn basic information about different Disney characters and view additional details related to them.
