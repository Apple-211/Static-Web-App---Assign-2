Snow White

Backstory

Snow White was born a royal princess, but her mother died shortly after her birth. Her father then remarried a cold and ruthless Queen. Following the death of her father, the orphaned Snow White was forced to dress in dirty rags and work as a maid by her jealous stepmother. Snow White ran away where she befriends seven miners, survives a deadly poison, and is ultimately rescued by true love

Role

Main Character/Royal Princess

Abilities

Resilient, Animal communication, Domestic Expertise, Superhuman Beauty

Personality

Optimistic, pure hearted, cheerful, and compassionate

First Appearance

1937 film Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs
